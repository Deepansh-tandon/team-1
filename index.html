You can run various types of campaigns including product launches, brand awareness, content creation, sponsored posts, reviews, giveaways, and more. The flexibility of our platform allows you to tailor your campaigns to meet your specific goals.

Payments are handled securely through our platform. Brands will be required to deposit funds for the collaboration, which will be held in escrow until the agreed-upon deliverables are met. Once the campaign is completed and approved, the payment will be released to the influencer.

Brands can browse through our database of influencers, select those who match their campaign needs, and initiate collaborations. Influencers can create profiles showcasing their reach, engagement, and previous collaborations, making it easy for brands to find the perfect match.
